ABOUT FLOWDESK PRO
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: What is FlowDesk Pro?
A: FlowDesk Pro is Acme Corporation's flagship workplace productivity platform.
   It combines project management, team communication, document collaboration,
   and AI-powered task automation into a single, unified dashboard. It is
   designed for teams of 5 to 5,000+ users across any industry.

Q: What platforms does FlowDesk Pro support?
A: FlowDesk Pro is available as:
   • Web app (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — latest 2 versions)
   • Desktop apps: Windows 10/11, macOS 12+, Ubuntu 20.04+
   • Mobile apps: iOS 16+ and Android 12+
   All platforms sync in real-time.

Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full Starter plan features.
   No credit card is required to start the trial.

Q: Do you charge per seat or per organisation?
A: FlowDesk Pro is priced per active user per month. A user is considered
   "active" if they log in at least once in the billing month. Guest users
   (view-only access) do not count toward the seat count.

Q: What happens when I exceed my user limit on Starter?
A: The system will prompt you to upgrade to the Growth plan before adding more
   users. No additional users can be added without upgrading.

Q: Is there a non-profit or educational discount?
A: Yes. Registered non-profits and accredited educational institutions receive
   a 40% discount on the Growth or Business plan. Contact sales@acmecorp.com
   with proof of status.

FEATURES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: What are the AI features in FlowDesk Pro?
A: Business and Enterprise plans include:
   • FlowAI Task Suggester: Analyses your project history and auto-suggests
     the next tasks, assignees, and due dates.
   • Smart Summaries: Generates meeting notes summaries from integrated
     video calls (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet).
   • Blockers Detector: Flags tasks at risk of missing deadlines.
   • Natural Language Search: Search all docs and tasks using plain English.

Q: Does FlowDesk Pro integrate with other tools?
A: Yes. Available integrations include:
   • Communication: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Chat
   • Email: Outlook, Gmail
   • Calendar: Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar
   • File storage: SharePoint, Google Drive, Dropbox Business, OneDrive
   • Development: GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Bitbucket
   • Finance: QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks
   Custom integrations are available via REST API and Webhooks.

Q: Is there an API available?
A: Yes. FlowDesk Pro offers a REST API (v3) and GraphQL API. Full documentation
   is available at https://developer.acmecorp.com. API access is included in
   Growth, Business, and Enterprise plans. Rate limits apply per plan.

SECURITY & COMPLIANCE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: Is FlowDesk Pro compliant with GDPR?
A: Yes. FlowDesk Pro is fully GDPR-compliant. Data Processing Agreements
   (DPAs) are available upon request. EU customers' data is stored exclusively
   in EU-based Azure data centres (West Europe region).

Q: What certifications does FlowDesk Pro hold?
A: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, HIPAA-eligible (Business & Enterprise), GDPR

Q: Is data encrypted?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

Q: What is the uptime guarantee?
A:
  • Starter / Growth : 99.5% monthly uptime
  • Business         : 99.9% monthly uptime

Q: What support options are available?
A:

   log in to your FlowDesk Pro dashboard → Help → Contact Support.
