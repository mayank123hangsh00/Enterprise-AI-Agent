ACME CORPORATION
IT SECURITY GUIDELINES
Version 5.1 | Effective: February 1, 2025
Information Security Team | it-security@acmecorp.com

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. PURPOSE & SCOPE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
These guidelines govern acceptable use of all information technology systems,
data, and network resources owned or leased by Acme Corporation. This policy
applies to all employees, contractors, interns, and third-party vendors with
access to Acme systems — regardless of location.

2. PASSWORD POLICY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2.1 Requirements
  • Minimum length          : 14 characters
  • Complexity             : Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, AND
                             special character (!@#$%^&*)
  • Maximum age            : Passwords must be changed every 90 days
  • Reuse restriction      : Last 12 passwords cannot be reused

2.2 Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  MFA is MANDATORY for:
  • VPN access
  • Corporate email (Outlook / Microsoft 365)
  • Access to cloud services (Azure Portal, AWS Console)
  • Any system marked as "Confidential" or "Restricted"
  Approved MFA methods: Microsoft Authenticator app, hardware security keys.
  SMS-based MFA is NOT permitted.

2.3 Password Managers
  All employees are provided a company-licensed 1Password account. Storing
  corporate passwords in browsers or personal password managers is prohibited.

3. DATA CLASSIFICATION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Level         | Label        | Examples                    | Encryption
  ──────────────|──────────────|─────────────────────────────|──────────
  1 – Public    | PUBLIC       | Marketing materials, website| Optional
  2 – Internal  | INTERNAL     | Internal memos, project docs| In transit
  3 – Confidential| CONF       | Customer PII, contracts     | In transit + rest
  4 – Restricted| RESTRICTED   | Board reports, source code  | Always + key mgmt

  All new documents must be labelled with the appropriate classification in
  the document header or file metadata before sharing or storage.

4. ACCEPTABLE USE OF COMPANY DEVICES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Permitted:
  • Work-related tasks and communication
  • Approved software from the company app catalogue (Software Center)
  • Incidental personal use during non-work hours (reasonable limits)

  Prohibited:
  • Installing unlicensed or non-approved software
  • Crypto mining, torrenting, or accessing illegal content
  • Connecting to unknown or public Wi-Fi without VPN active
  • Disabling security software (antivirus, EDR agents, disk encryption)
  • Using personal USB drives or storage devices (use company-approved devices)

5. VPN POLICY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • VPN (Cisco Secure Client) MUST be active whenever accessing internal
    systems outside the office network.
  • VPN credentials must not be shared. Each employee has a unique profile.
  • Split tunnelling is disabled — all traffic routes through the corporate VPN.
  • Approved VPN profiles are managed by IT Security; do not modify settings.

6. EMAIL & PHISHING
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Never open attachments or click links in unexpected emails.
  • Verify wire transfer or payment requests via phone — never email alone.
  • Report suspicious emails using the "Report Phishing" button in Outlook.
  • Acme IT will NEVER ask for your password over email or phone.
  • Phishing simulation exercises are conducted quarterly. Failures result in
    mandatory security re-training.

7. INCIDENT REPORTING
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Report ALL security incidents within 1 hour of discovery:
    • Security Hotline : +1 (800) 226-3767 ext. 9911  (24/7)
    • Email (urgent)   : security-incident@acmecorp.com
    • IT Portal        : https://ithelp.acmecorp.internal → "Security Incident"

  Examples of reportable incidents:
  • Lost or stolen device (laptop, phone, access card)
  • Suspected malware infection or ransomware
  • Unauthorised access to your account
  • Accidentally sending Confidential data to the wrong recipient
  • Clicking a phishing link

  The Information Security team will respond within 15 minutes for Critical
  incidents and within 2 hours for High severity incidents.

8. BRING YOUR OWN DEVICE (BYOD)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Personal devices may only access corporate email and Microsoft Teams via
  the approved Intune Company Portal app, which enforces:
  • Device encryption
  • Remote wipe capability
  • OS patch currency (no more than 30 days behind latest patch)
  BYOD devices may NOT access Confidential or Restricted data.

9. CLOUD & SaaS USAGE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Approved cloud services : Microsoft 365, Azure (company tenant), Slack,
                            Jira, Confluence, GitHub Enterprise, Figma
  Prohibited              : Uploading company data to personal Google Drive,
                            Dropbox personal, WeTransfer, or unapproved AI
                            tools (ChatGPT with data input, etc.)
  New SaaS procurement    : Submit a request via IT Security approval workflow
                            (allow 5 business days for security review).

10. POLICY VIOLATIONS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Violations are handled progressively:
  • First offence   : Verbal warning + mandatory training
  • Second offence  : Written warning on HR record
  • Third offence   : Disciplinary action up to and including termination
  Severe breaches (data exfiltration, wilful sabotage) skip progressive steps.
