ACME CORPORATION
REMOTE WORK POLICY
Version 2.4 | Effective: January 15, 2025
People & Culture Team | hr-helpdesk@acmecorp.com

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. PURPOSE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Acme Corporation supports flexible work arrangements as part of our commitment
to employee wellbeing and modern work practices. This policy establishes clear
expectations for employees who work remotely on a regular or occasional basis.

2. ELIGIBILITY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2.1 Who May Work Remotely
  Remote work (full or hybrid) is available to employees who:
  a) Have completed their 3-month probation period successfully.
  b) Hold a role classified as "remote-eligible" by their department head.
     (Roles requiring physical presence — facilities, reception, lab staff —
     are not eligible.)
  c) Have a consistent performance rating of 3/5 or above.
  d) Have received written approval from their direct manager.

2.2 Remote Work Arrangements
  Type            | Description
  ────────────────|──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Hybrid          | 2–3 days in office, rest remote (most common)
  Fully Remote    | Requires VP approval + quarterly in-person visits
  Occasional WFH  | Ad-hoc, approved day-by-day via manager
  Temporary WFH   | Short-term (e.g., illness recovery, home emergency)

3. EQUIPMENT & HOME WORKSPACE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1 Company-Provided Equipment
  The following are provided for approved remote workers:
  • Laptop (as per standard spec for the role)
  • USB-C dock station (for full-time remote employees)
  • Noise-cancelling headset
  • Webcam (if not built into laptop)

3.2 Employee Responsibilities
  • Maintain a safe, distraction-minimised workspace with a reliable internet
    connection (minimum 25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload).
  • Ensure children, pets, or household members do not disrupt video calls.
  • Company equipment must NOT be used by household members or third parties.
  • Equipment must be returned immediately upon departure from the company.

3.3 Ergonomics
  Employees are encouraged to complete the online Ergonomics Self-Assessment
  on the HR portal. An annual budget of $200 is available for ergonomic
  accessories (approved list on HR portal). Submit receipts via Concur.

4. COMMUNICATION & AVAILABILITY EXPECTATIONS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4.1 Core Hours
  Remote employees MUST be reachable during core hours: 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  in their team's primary time zone.

4.2 Response Time Standards
  • Microsoft Teams messages : Respond within 30 minutes during core hours
  • Emails                  : Respond within 4 hours during business hours
  • Urgent calls / pages    : Within 15 minutes during working hours

4.3 Meetings
  • Camera ON is expected for all internal and customer-facing video calls
    unless technical issues or health reasons prevent it.
  • Use a virtual background or ensure a professional-looking background.
  • Be punctual; late arrivals to meetings are noted as a performance factor.

4.4 Status Updates
  All remote employees must post a daily status in their team's Teams channel:
  • Start-of-day: "Online — available" + top 3 tasks for the day
  • End-of-day  : Brief summary of completed work + any blockers

5. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Expense                      | Limit  | Notes
  ─────────────────────────────|────────|─────────────────────────────────────
  Internet (home)              | $50/mo | Requires ISP invoice
  Ergonomic accessories (annual)| $200  | See approved list on HR portal
  Coworking / hot-desk space   | $150/mo| Requires receipts + manager approval
  Mobile data (if applicable)  | $30/mo | Only for field/travel roles

  Submit all expense claims via Concur within 30 days of the expense date.
  Claims older than 90 days will not be reimbursed.

6. DATA SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Remote employees are subject to all provisions in the IT Security Guidelines.
  Key requirements when working remotely:
  • VPN MUST be active whenever accessing internal systems or CONF/RESTRICTED
    data — no exceptions.
  • Do NOT use public Wi-Fi (cafes, airports, hotels) without VPN.
  • Lock screen when stepping away from the computer (Win+L / Cmd+Ctrl+Q).
  • Ensure screens are not visible to others in public or shared spaces.
  • Never print Confidential or Restricted documents on personal printers.

7. PERFORMANCE & ACCOUNTABILITY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Remote work is a privilege, not a right. It can be revoked if:
  • Attendance standards are not met (missed core hours without notice)
  • Performance drops below a 3/5 rating
  • The employee is unreachable during agreed working hours on two or more
    occasions within any 30-day period
  • Security policy violations occur

  Managers conduct a formal remote work arrangement review every 6 months.

8. TRAVELLING & TIME ZONE CHANGES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Notify your manager and HR if you intend to work from a different country
    or time zone for more than 10 consecutive days. Tax and legal implications
    may apply. HR will provide guidance.
  • Working abroad without prior approval may result in tax liability for both
    the employee and the company.

9. IN-PERSON REQUIREMENTS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Even fully remote employees are required to attend in person:
  • Quarterly all-hands / town hall meetings
  • Annual performance review meeting with manager
  • Team offsites (typically 1–2 per year)
  • New joiner onboarding week (first week is always in-office)
  Travel and accommodation costs for mandatory in-person events are covered
  by the company per the Travel Policy.
