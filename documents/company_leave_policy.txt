ACME CORPORATION
EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY
Version 3.2 | Effective: January 1, 2025
HR Department – Human Resources & People Operations

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. PURPOSE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This policy defines the types of leave available to all full-time employees of
Acme Corporation, the entitlements for each leave type, the approval process,
and the rules governing leave accrual and carry-forward.

2. SCOPE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This policy applies to all permanent full-time employees. Contract staff,
interns, and part-time employees are governed by separate addendums attached
to their employment contracts.

3. ANNUAL (PAID) LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1 Entitlement
  • Years 0–2 of service   : 18 working days per calendar year
  • Years 3–5 of service   : 22 working days per calendar year
  • Years 6+ of service    : 25 working days per calendar year

3.2 Accrual
  Leave accrues at a rate of 1.5 days per completed month of service.
  New joiners are eligible to apply for leave after completing 3 months of
  probation.

3.3 Carry-Forward
  A maximum of 10 unused annual leave days may be carried into the next
  calendar year. Leave exceeding this limit is forfeited and cannot be
  encashed unless the employee is exiting the company.

3.4 Application Process
  a) Employees must apply via the HR portal (hr.acmecorp.internal) at least
     5 working days before the intended leave start date.
  b) Leave of more than 10 consecutive days requires manager AND department
     head approval, and must be applied for at least 3 weeks in advance.
  c) Emergency leave requests (less than 5 days' notice) are at the manager's
     discretion and require written justification.

4. SICK LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
4.1 Entitlement
  All employees receive 12 paid sick days per calendar year.

4.2 Medical Certificate
  • 1–2 consecutive days  : Self-declaration via HR portal is sufficient.
  • 3+ consecutive days   : A medical certificate from a registered physician
                            must be submitted within 48 hours of return.

4.3 No Carry-Forward
  Unused sick leave does NOT carry forward and CANNOT be encashed.

4.4 Serious Illness
  Employees with a diagnosed serious illness (hospitalisation > 5 days) may
  apply for Extended Medical Leave (EML) of up to 60 working days per year,
  subject to HR and management approval. EML days are separate from standard
  sick leave.

5. MATERNITY LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Duration  : 26 weeks (182 calendar days) fully paid
  • Eligibility: Employees with at least 12 months of continuous service
  • Notice    : Submit expected due date to HR at least 8 weeks before the
                intended leave start date.
  • Extension : An unpaid extension of up to 12 weeks may be requested.

6. PATERNITY LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Duration  : 10 working days fully paid
  • Must be taken within 3 months of the child's birth/adoption date.
  • Eligibility: No minimum service requirement.

7. ADOPTION LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Primary caregiver   : 12 weeks fully paid (same rules as maternity leave)
  Secondary caregiver : 10 working days (same rules as paternity leave)

8. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Type of Loss                        | Paid Days
  ────────────────────────────────────|──────────
  Spouse / Life Partner               | 5 days
  Child (including stillbirth)        | 5 days
  Parent / Parent-in-law              | 3 days
  Sibling                             | 3 days
  Grandparent                         | 2 days
  Close friend (at manager's discretion)| 1 day

  Additional unpaid compassionate leave may be granted at manager discretion.

9. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Employees are entitled to all gazetted national public holidays. If a public
  holiday falls on a weekend, a substitute day will be given on the nearest
  weekday. The HR portal publishes the official list at the start of each year.

10. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Employees may apply for LWP for personal reasons after exhausting all paid
  leave balances. LWP beyond 5 consecutive days requires VP-level approval.
  Salary, benefits, and leave accrual are paused during LWP periods.

11. RECALL FROM LEAVE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  In exceptional business circumstances, the company may recall an employee
  from planned leave with a minimum of 72 hours' notice. Unused leave days
  caused by the recall will be reinstated in full.
