WELCOME FROM THE CEO
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Welcome to Acme Corporation! We are a technology-driven company committed to
building innovative solutions that make everyday workflows smarter and simpler.
This handbook outlines our culture, your rights and responsibilities, and the
standards we uphold together as a team.

1. OUR MISSION, VISION & VALUES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Mission : Empower teams worldwide with intelligent software that saves time.
Vision  : Become the world's most trusted workplace productivity platform.

Core Values:
  INTEGRITY     – We do what we say. We own our mistakes.
  INNOVATION    – We question the status quo and build boldly.
  COLLABORATION – Great ideas come from diverse, connected teams.
  CUSTOMER FIRST– Every decision starts with the customer outcome.
  INCLUSION     – Everyone deserves to feel they belong.

2. CODE OF CONDUCT
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2.1 Professional Behaviour
  All employees are expected to:
  • Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with dignity and respect.
  • Avoid any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
  • Protect confidential information (customer data, trade secrets, financials).
  • Never engage in conflicts of interest without prior written disclosure.

2.2 Zero Tolerance Policy
  The following will result in immediate termination without notice:
  • Sexual harassment or assault
  • Theft or fraud
  • Wilful damage to company property
  • Sharing customer PII with unauthorised parties
  • Workplace violence or threats thereof

2.3 Reporting Violations
  Report concerns via the anonymous Ethics Hotline: ethics@acmecorp.com
  or call +1 (800) 226-3767 ext. 1111. All reports are investigated within
  10 business days. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is prohibited.

3. WORKING HOURS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Standard Hours : Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (local office time)
Core Hours     : 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM (all teams must be reachable)
Lunch Break    : 1 hour (not counted in working hours)
Overtime       : Pre-approved overtime is compensated at 1.5× base rate.
Flexible Start : Employees may start between 8:00–10:00 AM with manager
                 agreement, as long as 8 hours are completed daily.

4. DRESS CODE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Day-to-day  : Smart casual (jeans acceptable; avoid gym/beachwear).
  Client visits: Business professional unless client culture differs.
  Casual Friday: Relaxed dress is permitted on Fridays (no shorts).
  Remote work  : No formal requirement, but professional attire expected
                 for video calls with customers or leadership.

5. COMPENSATION & PAYROLL
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Salaries are paid on the last working day of each month via direct deposit.
  • Pay slips are available on the HR portal by the 25th of each month.
  • Annual salary reviews occur every April, based on performance ratings.
  • Performance bonuses (if applicable) are paid in December.
  • Expense reimbursements are processed within 10 business days of submission.

6. BENEFITS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Health Benefits:
  • Comprehensive medical insurance (employee + spouse + up to 2 children)
  • Dental and vision coverage included
  • Mental health support: 12 free counselling sessions per year via EAP

Retirement:
  • Company matches 401(k) contributions up to 5% of base salary

Learning & Development:
  • Annual L&D budget: $1,500 per employee
  • Access to LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, and Pluralsight (company-paid)
  • 2 paid conference days per year

Wellness:
  • Gym reimbursement: up to $50/month
  • Monthly wellness day (last Friday of each month — half day off)
  • Free healthy snacks in all office kitchens

7. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Formal reviews: Mid-year (June) and Year-end (December)
  • Continuous feedback: Monthly 1:1s with direct manager are mandatory
  • Performance Improvement Plan (PIP): Triggered if rating falls below 2/5
    for two consecutive reviews. PIP duration is 90 days.
  • Promotion cycles: January and July each year

8. SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Do not share unreleased product information, financial data, or customer
    names on any social platform.
  • You may mention your employer on LinkedIn. Do not speak on behalf of
    Acme Corporation without explicit comms team approval.
  • Common sense rule: Would you be comfortable if the CEO saw this post?

9. ONBOARDING & PROBATION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Probation period: 3 months for all new hires.
  • A buddy is assigned on Day 1 for first 4 weeks of guidance.
  • 30-60-90 day plan agreed with manager in Week 1.
  • Formal probation review in Week 11 with HR present.

10. OFF-BOARDING
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • Notice period: 30 days (staff) / 60 days (managers & above)
  • Exit interview with HR required for all voluntary departures
  • Return all company assets (laptop, badge, access cards) on last day
  • IT access is revoked on last working day at 6:00 PM
